This Privacy Policy explains how The Agilists Private Limited ("The Agilists", "we", "us" or "our") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you visit agilists.co.uk or get in touch with us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in an open and transparent manner in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

The Agilists Private Limited is the "data controller" responsible for your personal data.

2. The information we collect

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

Information you give us

  • Contact enquiries: when you complete our contact form or email us, we collect your name, work email address, company name, the service you are interested in, and the content of your message.

Information we collect automatically

  • Technical & usage data: if you consent to analytics cookies, we collect information such as your approximate location, device and browser type, pages viewed, and how you interact with the site. See our Cookie Policy for details.
  • Server logs: our hosting provider may automatically log your IP address and request data for security and reliability purposes.

We do not intentionally collect special category data (such as health, ethnicity, or political views). Please do not include such information in your enquiries.

Under UK GDPR, we must have a lawful basis for processing your personal data. We rely on the following:

6. International transfers

Some of our providers are based outside the UK. Where we transfer personal data internationally, we ensure an appropriate safeguard is in place, such as the UK International Data Transfer Agreement, the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses, or reliance on a country the UK Government has deemed to provide adequate protection.

7. How long we keep your data

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Enquiries that do not lead to an engagement: typically up to 24 months, after which they are deleted.
  • Client records: for the duration of our engagement and for up to 6 years afterwards to meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations.
  • Analytics data: as set out in our Cookie Policy.

8. Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • be informed about how your data is used;
  • access a copy of the personal data we hold about you;
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten");
  • restrict or object to our processing;
  • data portability; and
  • withdraw consent at any time where we rely on it.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@agilists.co.uk. We will respond within one month. You will not usually have to pay a fee.

9. How to complain

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, please contact us first so we can try to resolve them. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's supervisory authority, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), at ico.org.uk or on 0303 123 1113.

10. Security

We use appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

11. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to third-party sites. This policy does not apply to those sites, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before providing any personal data.

12. Children

Our website and services are intended for businesses and are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

13. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be highlighted on this page.

14. Contact us

For any questions about this policy or your personal data, contact us at privacy@agilists.co.uk.
